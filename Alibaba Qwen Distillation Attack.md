# Alibaba Qwen Distillation Attack

## What Happened
Anthropic alleged that **Alibaba** orchestrated a nation-state-level model-extraction campaign from **22 April to 5 June 2026** using approximately **25,000 fraudulent accounts**. The operation stole Claude's software engineering and agentic reasoning capabilities through **28.8 million API queries**.

## Why It Matters
- Largest known model extraction campaign.
- Confirms API-only capability theft at massive scale with zero infrastructure access.
- Triggered U.S. Commerce Department restrictions.
