# Illinois AI Safety Audit Mandate

## What Happened
Illinois Governor JB Pritzker signed the **Artificial Intelligence Safety Measures Act**, effective **January 1, 2027**. It requires frontier AI model developers to undergo annual independent third party safety audits, report critical incidents within **72 hours**, evaluate and mitigate catastrophic risks, and maintain accurate public disclosures. Penalties reach **$1M** for first offences and **$3M** for repeat violations.

## Why It Matters
- First U.S. state law mandating independent audits of frontier models.
- Shifts burden of proof to AI companies and creates real financial consequences.
- May influence federal and international regulation.
