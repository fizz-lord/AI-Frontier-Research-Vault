# First Confirmed AI Generated Zero Day Exploit

## What Happened
**Google Threat Intelligence** confirmed in **May 2026** that an AI system discovered a novel semantic logic flaw in production authentication and generated a functional Python exploit deployed against real infrastructure. The threat actor achieved breakout in **29 minutes**.

## Why It Matters
- Qualitatively new attack class.
- AI assisted zero day discovery faster than human researchers.
- Signals a shift to AI enabled exploitation as standard threat model.

## Notes
- Vulnerability lifecycle is accelerating.
- Defenders should expect AI generated exploit variants for disclosed bugs.
