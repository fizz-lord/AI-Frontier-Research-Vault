# EU AI Act Article 50 Compliance Deadline

## What Happened
The **EU AI Act Article 50** mandatory transparency obligations became enforceable on **2 August 2026**. Chatbots and generative AI systems must disclose AI interaction and mark AI generated content in machine readable format, establishing the first binding global **right to know** principle.

## Why It Matters
- Global regulatory inflection point that also applies to non-EU companies serving EU users.
- Creates precedent that contrasts with U.S. state by state patchwork and Chinese extraction strategies.
- Forces providers to expose AI-generated content and interaction boundaries.
