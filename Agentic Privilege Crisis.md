# Agentic Privilege Crisis

## What Happened
A 2026 breach at a major legal research provider exposed data across 21,000 enterprise customers after a single over-permissioned automated AI account read nearly every secret accessible to it. Dark Reading and Bloomberg Law documented how AI agents are routinely granted broader access than human users, with static identity controls that never adapt. Meta infrastructure VP warned at VB Transform that agentic queries grew 30 times in a single half, breaking 20 year old access assumptions.

## Why It Matters
- Shift from human operated accounts to persistent autonomous non human identities creates a new attack surface.
- You cannot phish an AI agent, but you can over privilege one.
- One misconfigured agent becomes a direct line to an organisation's entire data estate with no human in the loop.
