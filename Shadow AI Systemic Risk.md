# Shadow AI as Systemic Enterprise Risk

## What Happened
Infosecurity analysis reveals **shadow AI** -- AI systems deployed by business units without security awareness -- has become a widespread organisational reality in 2026. Employees upload sensitive data to unsecured public services, create untracked integrations, and bypass governance controls. Traditional firewalls and IAM do not address this threat model.

## Why It Matters
- Shifts security paradigm: the perimeter is now defined by AI governance, not network boundaries.
- Creates systemic risk across organisations that treat AI adoption as business enablement rather than security.
- Requires new detection, classification, and access control frameworks.
