# US Jailbreak Benchmark After Anthropic Flaw

## What Happened
Amazon researchers discovered a jailbreaking flaw in **Claude Fable 5** that could cause guardrails to fail and produce vulnerability exploitation code. The discovery triggered **Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent** involvement, export controls, and government imposed model evaluations. Anthropic worked with Amazon, Google, and Microsoft to create an industry wide standardised jailbreak benchmark measuring **outcome severity** rather than mere possibility.

## Why It Matters
- Signals unprecedented government oversight on frontier models.
- New benchmark shifts testing from existence of jailbreak to severity of impact.
- Shows rapid U.S. policy course correction and multi vendor coordination.
