# AI Safety Index Regression

## What Happened
The Future of Life Institute's latest **AI Safety Index** reports that leading AI companies have weakened key safety commitments even as model capabilities increase. **Anthropic** ranked highest with only a **C+**; **OpenAI** and **Google** received **Cs**; **xAI**, **DeepSeek**, and **Mistral** received failing grades. The report describes this as **moving the goalposts**.

## Why It Matters
- Counterintuitive finding: safety commitments decline as capabilities increase, suggesting misaligned incentives.
- Indicates industry is prioritising competitive advantage over established safety frameworks.
- May trigger regulatory response if private safety standards continue eroding.
