# OpenAI Government Stake Ownership

## What Happened
OpenAI pitched the White House on a **5% government stake** worth roughly **$42 billion**, and wants other AI labs to offer the same cut.

## Why It Matters
- If compute, models, and data sit under government linked ownership, supply chain trust becomes political.
- Sovereign interest in a model owner can change disclosure, export, or surveillance decisions.
- Indirect path to broader societal risk if ownership converts to distributed benefit mechanisms.

## Notes
- Strategic rather than purely technical
- Relevance to AI security lies in model governance and trust boundaries
- Watch for policy outcomes and model access rules
