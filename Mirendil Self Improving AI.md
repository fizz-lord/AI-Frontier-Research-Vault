# Mirendil and Self Improving AI Research

## What Happened
Two ex Anthropic researchers raised **$200 million** for **Mirendil**, targeting a self improving AI research loop.

## Why It Matters
- Self improvement widens the gap between capability growth and safety validation.
- If the research loop is lab private, publication and independent audit are reduced.
- Market appetite for recursive improvement is rising.

## Notes
- Track capability milestones versus disclosure cadence
- Evaluate whether research loop outputs are externally assessable
