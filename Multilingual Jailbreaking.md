# Multilingual Jailbreaking of LLM Safety Guardrails

## What Happened
A Forbes feature documented how switching prompts to lower-resource languages bypassed LLM safety guardrails, showing that safety alignment is disproportionately English-centric.

## Why It Matters
- If guardrail effectiveness depends on language training coverage, multilingual deployment widens attack surface.
- Offensive teams can probe frontier models systematically through under-resourced language variants.
- Relevant to any system serving non-English user bases without language-aware safety layers.
