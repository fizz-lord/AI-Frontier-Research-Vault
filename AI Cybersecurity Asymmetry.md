# AI Cybersecurity Asymmetry

## What Happened
Security commentary concluded that AI makes **attackers cheaper** and **defenders pickier**. Local models matter most; the real win is better prioritisation, not full automation.

## Why It Matters
- Defender advantage depends on judgement, not volume.
- Automation alone does not remove analyst bias or alert noise.
- Local models reduce cloud trust exposure for sensitive security workloads.

## Notes
- Security teams should prioritise triage, enrichment, and decision support
- Watch local model security versus centralised model risk
