# Alibaba Distillation Campaign Against Anthropic

## What Happened
Anthropic reported to the U.S. Senate that **Alibaba's Qwen AI team** orchestrated a campaign using **~25,000 fraudulent accounts** to generate **28.8 million interactions** with Claude, extracting model knowledge to improve competing technology. The campaign exploited Anthropic's API access policies.

## Why It Matters
- Demonstrates industrial scale model extraction attacks targeting frontier models.
- Shows how data privacy and extraction threats operate at massive scope with sophisticated credential forgery.
- Triggered Anthropic's export controls and heightened U.S. to China AI tensions.
