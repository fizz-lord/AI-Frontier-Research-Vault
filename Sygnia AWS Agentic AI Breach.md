# Sygnia AWS Agentic AI Breach

## What Happened
Sygnia research reveals a lone threat actor used AI workflows to breach a large AWS customer, execute extortion, and perform reconnaissance, lateral movement, and privilege escalation in **72 hours** -- work that typically requires weeks of human effort. The attacker exploited gaps in secrets management, identity governance, and cloud permissions, adapting tactics in real time.

## Why It Matters
- Illustrates how accessible agentic AI enables resource constrained attackers to scale sophisticated cloud attacks.
- Shifts the threat landscape: defenders now face both nation state sophistication and lower entry barriers for individual actors.
