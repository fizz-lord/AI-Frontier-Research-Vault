# UBTECH U1 Identity Replication Risk

## What Happened
UBTECH’s **U1 humanoid robots** gained silicone skin, emotion reading, memory, and custom human replica options.

## Why It Matters
- Memory and custom replicas expand biometric and identity attack surface.
- Emotion reading creates continuous sensitive behavioural capture.
- Humanoid presence in homes or offices increases physical and data access.

## Notes
- Treat biometric data from humanoids as high sensitivity
- Assess custom replica misuse for social engineering and fraud
