# Chinese Open Weight LLMs and Capability Gap

## What Happened
Chinese firms released open weight models, including **GLM 5.2** and **Zhipu AI**, matching or exceeding U.S. frontier model performance on benchmarks. **360 Security** released **Tulongfeng**, an AI powered vulnerability discovery tool claiming **3,400+ flaws** found -- positioned as China's answer to Anthropic's Mythos. Open weights enable defenders and attackers to locally experiment with jailbreaks and offensive use.

## Why It Matters
- Narrows U.S. technological advantage in frontier AI.
- Open weight models democratise attack capabilities while also providing defensive options.
- Geopolitical competition may accelerate capability at the expense of safety standards.
